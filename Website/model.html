Our AI Model

At the heart of PlantDoc lies our advanced AI model, designed to diagnose plant diseases with unprecedented accuracy.

Model Architecture

Our model is built using TensorFlow and Keras, leveraging the pre-trained VGG16 model, a state-of-the-art neural network architecture for image recognition. It processes images with a batch size of 32 and an input size of 224 x 224 pixels across 33 different plant diseases.

The model has been trained for 50 epochs on a dataset that includes over 56,000 images spanning 33 distinct plant diseases. This extensive training has enabled our model to achieve an impressive 97.6% accuracy in identifying a wide range of plant diseases.
